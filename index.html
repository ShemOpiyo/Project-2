ABOUT RIYADH

Riyadh is a young-youth group that came up together with a way of saving to sustain their livelihood of individual so that at the end of every year every individual has something in his/her pocket. The saving of the money is on a yearly basis. Group members are required to attend a meetin which is done at least once at the end of every week.

At the end of every year, Riyadh members conduct a party to thank themselves for sacrificing themselves for a whole year for saving their small income which interns make them know that one can actually become a millionare even if he/she decides to save his/her small money.

WHAT WE DO

Each individual save some small amount of money on a yearly basis. At the end of the year a small party is organised so that people can appreciate their good work and the sacrifice they have made for the whole year
